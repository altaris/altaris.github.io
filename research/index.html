  • Trust your neighbours: handling noise in multi-objective optimisation using kNN-averaging, 2021, with Stefan Klikovits, Ahmet Cetinkaya, and Paolo Arcaini. Submitted.

  • Opetopic algebras III: Presheaf models of homotopy-coherent opetopic algebras, 2020, with Chaitanya Leena Subramaniam. HAL mirror.

  • Syntactic approaches to opetopes, 2019, with Pierre-Louis Curien and Samuel Mimram. Accepted at Higher Structures. HAL mirror. Short version about the unnamed approach accepted and presented at Category Theory (CT) 2019.

  • A sequent calculus for opetopes, 2019, with Pierre-Louis Curien and Samuel Mimram. In LICS’19: Proceedings of the 34th Annual ACM/IEEE Symposium on Logic in Computer Science.

  • The equivalence between opetopic sets and many-to-one polygraphs, 2018. HAL mirror. Extended abstract accepted and presented at Higher-Dimensional Rewriting and Algebra (HDRA/FLOC) 2018.
